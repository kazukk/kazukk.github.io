The Art of Yokan

Yokan has been a beloved Japanese confection for centuries, evolving from a savory dish in China to the sweet delicacy we know today. Our yokan is crafted in Kyoto by the Mochizuki family, who have been perfecting their art for over 150 years.

The process begins with carefully selected azuki beans, prized for their superior flavor and texture. These beans are slowly cooked and blended with sugar to create a smooth paste. Agar, a natural gelatin derived from seaweed, is then added to give the yokan its distinctive firm yet silky texture.

What sets our yokan apart is the balance of sweetness and the pure flavor of azuki beans. It's an exemplary representation of the Japanese confectionery principle of letting natural flavors shine through minimal intervention.

Perfect as a gift or as an accompaniment to green tea, our yokan offers a true taste of Japanese culinary tradition.
